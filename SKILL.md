## Overview

Generate high-retention video scripts for YouTube long-form, TikTok short-form, and Instagram Reels. This skill produces four video content assets — a YouTube tutorial/explainer, a TikTok viral hook script, a Reels promotional clip, and a full Video Content Calendar — designed to capture attention in the first 3 seconds, hold it through the end, and convert viewers into followers, leads, or buyers.

Video is the highest-reach, lowest-cost marketing channel available to small businesses in 2026. A single viral TikTok can generate more leads than 6 months of paid ads. Yet most businesses don't post video because scripting is hard — they don't know the hooks, the structure, or the call to action. This skill solves that in under 5 minutes.

## Prompts

### Prompt 1: YouTube Tutorial/Explainer Script (8-12 Minutes)
Generate a complete YouTube video script optimized for watch time and SEO: a 30-second hook (problem/curiosity/shock stat), 60-second intro (credibility + promise + subscribe CTA), 6-8 content sections with b-roll suggestions and on-screen text cues, a mid-video engagement CTA (comment/like), a 90-second outro (summary + next video tease + subscribe push), and a video description template (first 150 chars + keyword-rich body + timestamps + links). Includes thumbnail concept (title text + visual), chapter markers, and optimal video length for the niche.

### Prompt 2: TikTok Hook & Short-Form Script (30-90 Seconds)
Generate 3 TikTok script variations for the same topic: a curiosity hook ("The mistake most [profession] make..."), a transformation hook ("I went from X to Y in Z days..."), and a controversy hook ("Unpopular opinion: [industry truth]..."). Each script: 30-90 seconds, written word-for-word, includes pacing notes (fast-cut vs slow-burn), on-screen text overlay suggestions, trending sound/audio category recommendation, and hashtag set (15 tags: 3 niche, 3 mid, 3 broad, 3 location, 3 viral). Includes best-post-time guide by niche and day.

### Prompt 3: Instagram Reels Promotional Script (15-60 Seconds)
Generate an Instagram Reels script for a product, service, or offer: 3-second hook (visual + text), problem agitation (5-7 seconds), solution reveal (10-15 seconds), social proof flash (5 seconds), and CTA with urgency (5 seconds). Includes caption template (first-line hook + body + CTA + hashtags), cover frame description, collab/duet strategy, and a Stories version that drives to the Reel. Optimized for the Instagram algorithm (save + share triggers baked in).

### Prompt 4: 30-Day Video Content Calendar
Generate a 30-day video content calendar across YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram Reels: 4 YouTube videos (weekly), 20 TikToks (daily Mon-Fri), and 8 Reels (2x/week). For each video: title, hook, 3-sentence content summary, platform, format (tutorial/promo/social proof/trending audio), and estimated production time. Includes a batching strategy (film all 4 YouTube in one day, 10 TikToks in another), a repurposing map (1 YouTube → 3 TikToks → 2 Reels → 1 LinkedIn post), and a "minimum viable video" tier for busy weeks (1 video/week that covers all platforms).

## Inputs Required

- **Business name and type** (e.g., "Peak Performance Gym, personal training studio in Las Vegas")
- **Target audience** (who watches and why they'd care)
- **Core offer or topic** (what the video promotes or teaches)
- **Brand voice** (energetic, authoritative, relatable, luxury, casual)
- **Call to action goal** (book a call, visit website, follow, buy a product)
- **Video topic or title idea** (optional — skill will generate one if blank)
