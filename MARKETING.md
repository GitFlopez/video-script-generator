# Marketing & Revenue Strategy: Video Script Generator

## ClawHub Marketplace Listing

**Title:** Video Script Generator — YouTube, TikTok & Instagram Reels

**Tagline:** Write high-retention video scripts for 3 platforms in 5 minutes. Hooks, b-roll cues, captions, hashtags, and a 30-day calendar included.

**Description:**
Video is the highest-reach, lowest-cost marketing channel available to small businesses in 2026. A single TikTok can generate more leads than 6 months of paid ads. But most businesses never post video because scripting is hard.

This skill solves that. Four prompts generate everything you need: a complete YouTube tutorial script (8-12 min with hook, intro, chapters, CTAs, thumbnail concept, and SEO description), 3 TikTok hook variations (curiosity, transformation, controversy — all with hashtag sets and timing data), an Instagram Reels promotional script (structured for maximum saves and shares), and a 30-day content calendar with batching strategy and repurposing map.

Works for: real estate agents, fitness coaches, restaurant owners, SaaS companies, e-commerce brands, consultants, course creators — any business that wants to build an audience on video without spending hours staring at a blank page.

**What you get:**
- Full YouTube script (hook + intro + body sections + mid-video CTA + outro + description template + thumbnail concept)
- 3 TikTok scripts with hooks, pacing notes, text overlays, hashtags, and optimal post times
- Instagram Reels script with frame-by-frame direction, caption template, Stories cross-promotion plan
- 30-day content calendar: 4 YouTube + 20 TikToks + 8 Reels = 32 content pieces
- Batching strategy (produce a month of content in 3 days)
- Repurposing map (1 YouTube = 8 content pieces)
