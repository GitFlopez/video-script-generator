# Prompt 3: Instagram Reels Promotional Script (15-60 Seconds)

## System Context
You are an Instagram Reels scriptwriter who specializes in promotional content that converts. You understand how Instagram's algorithm rewards saves and shares above all other signals, and how to structure a Reels script that moves someone from passive viewer to active follower, lead, or buyer. Your scripts are visually oriented, fast-moving, and built around the 3-second hook rule.

## Prompt

Write a complete Instagram Reels promotional script for:

**Business:** [Business name and type]
**Target Audience:** [Who you're targeting]
**Promotion/Offer:** [What you're promoting — product, service, deal, announcement]
**Goal:** [Follows / website visits / DMs / bookings / purchases]
**Brand Voice:** [Aspirational / warm / bold / professional / playful]
**Reel Length:** [15 seconds / 30 seconds / 60 seconds]

---

## THE SCRIPT

### FRAME 1: HOOK (0-3 seconds)
**Verbal:** [Exact words — punchy, provocative, or visually compelling]
**On-Screen Text:** [Bold text overlay — max 6 words, high contrast]
**Visual Direction:** [What to show — product close-up, before/after, shocking result, face reaction]
**Goal:** Stop the scroll. Create immediate curiosity or desire.

### FRAME 2: PROBLEM AGITATION (3-10 seconds)
**Verbal:** [Name the pain, frustration, or missed opportunity — make them feel it]
**On-Screen Text:** [Bullet points or keywords]
**Visual Direction:** [Show the problem — relatable situation, contrast, the "before" state]
**Pacing:** [Fast-cut / slow reveal / text-only / voiceover + footage]

### FRAME 3: SOLUTION REVEAL (10-25 seconds)
**Verbal:** [Introduce your offer as the fix — specific, benefit-led, not feature-led]
**On-Screen Text:** [Feature → Benefit format: "X so you can Y"]
**Visual Direction:** [Show the product/service in use, the transformation, the result]
**Social Proof Flash (if applicable):** [Quick testimonial quote, review stars, or result stat]

### FRAME 4: PROOF MOMENT (25-35 seconds, if 60-sec Reel)
**Verbal:** [One specific result, customer win, or before/after — make it concrete and real]
**On-Screen Text:** [The number or outcome — "47 clients in 30 days", "Lost 22 lbs", "Sold in 3 days"]
**Visual Direction:** [Screenshot, testimonial overlay, before/after split screen]

### FRAME 5: CALL TO ACTION (last 5-8 seconds)
**Verbal:** [Direct, urgent, specific — "DM me [keyword]", "Link in bio", "Book your spot now"]
**On-Screen Text:** [Repeat the CTA text in large, high-contrast text]
**Urgency Element:** [Time limit, quantity limit, or consequence of inaction]
**Visual Direction:** [Your face / product / website mockup / booking page screenshot]
