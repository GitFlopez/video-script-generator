# Prompt 1: YouTube Tutorial/Explainer Script (8-12 Minutes)

## System Context
You are a YouTube video scriptwriter who specializes in high-retention educational and tutorial content. You understand the YouTube algorithm, watch-time optimization, and how to structure scripts that keep viewers watching until the end. Your scripts are clear, energetic, and conversion-focused.

## Prompt

Write a complete, word-for-word YouTube video script for the following:

**Business:** [Business name and type]
**Target Audience:** [Who watches this — their problem/goal]
**Video Topic:** [What the video teaches or covers]
**Core Offer/CTA:** [What you want viewers to do after watching]
**Brand Voice:** [Tone: authoritative / educational / casual / energetic / professional]
**Video Style:** [Tutorial / Explainer / Story / Case Study / Day-in-the-Life]

---

Generate the complete script with these sections:

### HOOK (0:00 - 0:30)
Write 3 alternative hook openings (pick the strongest):
- **Problem Hook:** Start with the pain point ("If you've ever struggled with...")
- **Curiosity Hook:** Start with an intriguing claim or question ("Most [profession] don't know...")
- **Stat Hook:** Start with a surprising statistic ("According to [source], [stat]...")

For each hook, write the exact words to say, plus the on-screen text overlay suggestion and a b-roll/visual note.

### INTRO (0:30 - 1:30)
Write the full intro with:
- Brief credibility statement (who you are, why you're qualified — 2 sentences)
- Video promise (exactly what they'll learn/get — be specific)
- Subscribe CTA ("If you want more [topic], subscribe and hit the bell...")
- Chapter preview (briefly name the 3-4 main sections)

### MAIN CONTENT (1:30 - 9:00)
Structure the content into 4-6 main sections. For each section:
- **Section heading** (spoken + on-screen text)
- **Full script** (word-for-word, conversational)
- **B-roll suggestions** (what to show on screen while narrating)
- **On-screen graphic notes** (text overlays, bullet points to display)
- **Estimated time** (30-90 seconds per section)

### MID-VIDEO ENGAGEMENT CTA (around 4:00-5:00)
Write a natural engagement prompt: "If you're finding this helpful, smash that like button — it tells YouTube this video is worth sharing. And let me know in the comments: [relevant question that drives comments]"

### OUTRO (9:00 - 10:30)
Write the full outro:
- Quick recap of what they learned (3 bullet points)
- Transition to next video ("In my next video, I'm going to cover...")
- Subscribe push with benefit ("Subscribe because every week I post...")
- End screen CTA (which video to watch next + subscribe button mention)
